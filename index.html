What is Maths-Whizz?

Two tools, one platform

Maths-Whizz gives schools two complementary products, both included in the same platform.

Product 1

The Virtual Tutor

An AI-driven adaptive maths tutor that works one-to-one with each student. It assesses their level, sets a personalised learning path, adapts in real time as they progress, and builds confidence by filling gaps before moving on. Students get the equivalent of individual attention every session, without any extra work for the teacher.

Product 2

The Teachers' Resource

A ready-to-use library of interactive animated lessons, printable worksheets, enrichment tasks, and classroom activities, all organised by topic and year group. Teachers can plan, deliver, and differentiate maths lessons quickly, with no need to build materials from scratch.

How the Virtual Tutor works

Think of it as a teaching assistant for every child in your school

The challenge

Every child is at a different level. One teacher can't teach to all of them.

Research shows there's typically a four-year learning gap in a single classroom. Every pupil has different strengths, different gaps, a different maths age. One teacher, pitching lessons at the middle, will always leave some behind.

1 in 4 Year 6 pupils don't meet the expected standard in maths (DfE, 2024)

Step 1: Assess

Imagine asking a TA to assess every child individually

You'd want them to sit with each pupil, understand exactly where they are in maths, and identify every strength and weakness. Maths-Whizz does this automatically on day one: an adaptive assessment for your whole class, at once.

Step 2: Review

Then show you every pupil's gaps, topic by topic

Your TA would report back in detail: not just an overall score, but a granular picture across Place Value, Fractions, Decimals, Measures and more. Maths-Whizz gives you that view for every child in your class, after every session.

Step 3: Teach

Then deliver a tailored programme for each child

You'd ask your TA to give each pupil lessons matched to their individual needs, filling gaps, building confidence, preparing them for success in the classroom. Maths-Whizz does this simultaneously for every child, with animated, interactive lessons at exactly their level.

Step 4: Progress

And show you how the gaps are closing

Grey shows where each pupil started. Blue shows the gaps Maths-Whizz has since closed. As each child works through their personalised programme, those gaps fill in. Every pupil moving forward, session by session, with no marking.

The challenge for any teacher is to consistently give extra attention to every student within the school day. Our mission

Our journey

Founded in Kington, our story began with a simple goal: to help schools access technology that genuinely enhances learning. What started as a local initiative has grown into partnerships with leading EdTech companies across the UK, connecting innovative solutions with schools that want real results.

Our journey continues as we work to bridge the gap between technology and teaching, ensuring every school can benefit from tools that inspire both educators and learners.

Who we serve

We support UK school decision-makers and teachers who are adopting EdTech solutions to enhance learning. From individual teachers to whole-school implementations, we provide the support you need.
